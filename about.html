About CSSS

Building community and celebrating heritage at UVA

What We Do

CSSS serves as a vibrant platform for fostering connections among Chinese students, scholars, and the broader Charlottesville community. We organize a wide range of events, including cultural celebrations, social gatherings, academic workshops, and professional development activities.

Our programs aim to help members adapt to life at UVA and in the United States, celebrate Chinese culture, and build lasting friendships. Additionally, we act as a bridge between the Chinese community and the university administration to address concerns and improve the well-being of our members.

  • Cultural celebrations and traditional festivals
  • Social gatherings and community events
  • Academic workshops and study support
  • Professional development activities
  • New student orientation and campus guidance
  • Bridge between community and university administration

Our Mission

CSSS strives to create a welcoming and supportive environment for Chinese international students and scholars at UVA. Our mission is to promote cultural exchange, strengthen community bonds, and provide resources that enable personal and professional growth.

By encouraging diversity, collaboration, and cultural awareness, we aim to enhance the overall experience of our members and contribute positively to the multicultural fabric of UVA and Charlottesville.

Our History

Foundation & Growth

CSSS was established to address the unique needs of Chinese students and scholars at UVA. Over the years, we have grown from a small group of students to a thriving organization that serves hundreds of community members.

Our organization has played a crucial role in helping new students adjust to life in Charlottesville, providing both practical support and cultural connection.

Community Impact

Today, CSSS continues to be a bridge between Chinese culture and the broader UVA community. We organize cultural events that are open to all students, fostering cross-cultural understanding and appreciation.

Our alumni network spans across various industries and continues to support current students through mentorship and career guidance.

Our Values

Community

Building strong connections among Chinese students and scholars at UVA, creating a supportive network that lasts beyond graduation.

Cultural Heritage

Celebrating and preserving Chinese culture while sharing it with the broader UVA community through festivals and events.

Academic Excellence

Supporting students' academic journey through community connections and resource sharing that help students thrive at UVA.
