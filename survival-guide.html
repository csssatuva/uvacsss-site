Common First-Year Challenges

üó£Ô∏è Language & Communication

Tips for improving English and academic communication

  • Attend English conversation groups
  • Practice academic writing at the Writing Center
  • Don't hesitate to ask professors to repeat or clarify
  • Join study groups to practice discussion skills
  • Use office hours for one-on-one communication practice

üè† Homesickness & Cultural Adjustment

Strategies for managing homesickness and cultural differences

  • Stay connected with family through video calls
  • Find Chinese restaurants and grocery stores
  • Celebrate Chinese holidays with CSSS
  • Be open to new experiences and American culture
  • Seek counseling support if feeling overwhelmed

üí∞ Budget Management

Managing finances as an international student

  • Track expenses using apps like Mint or Excel
  • Take advantage of student discounts
  • Cook meals to save money on dining
  • Buy used textbooks or rent when possible
  • Understand currency exchange and transfer fees
