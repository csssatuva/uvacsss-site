Leadership Team

Meet the dedicated students leading CSSS and serving our community

主席团 Executive Board

主席团由四位成员组成，他们是社团的核心领导团队，负责全面监督社团的日常运作与战略发展。主席团不仅协调各部门之间的合作，确保活动顺利进行，还代表社团与外部机构、合作伙伴进行沟通与对接。他们致力于为社团成员创造一个有凝聚力、充满活力的环境，带领大家共同成长。

The executive board consists of four leaders who oversee the organization's daily operations and strategic growth. They facilitate collaboration between departments, ensure events run smoothly, and represent the organization in external communications with partners and institutions. Dedicated to fostering a cohesive and dynamic community, they lead with vision and passion, guiding all members toward growth and success.

Departments

活动部 Department of Activity

活动部是社团的"创意引擎"，负责策划与执行社团的各类活动，包括中秋晚会、春节联欢、校园好声音等大型活动，以及春季趣味小活动等。他们用热情与创意为成员们打造难忘的体验，让每一次活动都成为社团文化的亮点，增强成员之间的凝聚力与归属感。

The Department of Activity is the creative powerhouse behind all our activities, planning and executing a variety of events, including Mid-Autumn Festival Celebrations, Chinese New Year Gala, and talent showcases like UVA Voice, as well as fun seasonal activities. Their passion and creativity bring unforgettable experiences to our community, fostering a true sense of belonging for the Chinese international community at UVA.

宣信部 Department of Publicity

宣信部是社团的"形象设计师"，负责社团的视觉呈现与品牌传播。从活动照片拍摄、网站运营到周边商品（Merch）设计，宣信部用创意与技术打造社团的独特形象。他们还管理社交媒体账号，通过线上平台与夏村华人群体互动，让社团的故事被更多人看见与分享。

The Department of Publicity shapes our organization's public image and digital presence. They manage event photography, social media platform updates, website operations, and merchandise design, ensuring our identity is vibrant and recognizable. Through engaging social media content, they connect with the UVA Asian community online, sharing our stories and building a stronger, more connected network.

职发部 Department of Career Development

职发部专注于为社团成员及UVA华人学生群体提供职业发展的支持与资源。他们定期举办简历修改工作坊（Resume Workshop）、职业分享会（Career Panel）等活动，帮助大家提升职场竞争力。无论是校内资源整合还是与外部企业的合作，职发部都致力于为华人学生群体的未来职业道路铺平道路。

The Department of Career Development is committed to supporting our members and the UVA Chinese student community in their professional growth. They organize Resume Workshops, Career Panels, and networking events to enhance career readiness. Whether connecting students with on-campus resources or fostering partnerships with industry professionals, they strive to open doors for future career opportunities.

财务部 Department of Finance

财务部是社团的"财务管家"，负责管理社团的所有资金流动与预算规划。从活动经费的分配到长期财务规划，财务部确保每一笔资金都得到合理使用，为社团的各项活动提供坚实的后勤保障。他们用细致的财务管理支持社团的可持续发展，让每一份资源都发挥最大价值。

The Department of Finance serves as the organization's financial steward, managing all funding and budgeting needs. From allocating event expenses to long-term financial planning, they ensure that every dollar is used efficiently to support our initiatives. With meticulous financial oversight, they provide a solid foundation for sustainable development, maximizing the impact of our resources.

外联部 Department of Outreach

外联部是社团与外界沟通的桥梁，负责与赞助商、学校、大使馆以及其他校内外社团建立并维护合作关系。他们通过高效的沟通与协调，为社团争取资源与机会，扩大社团的影响力。外联部的努力让社团的活动更加丰富多彩，也为成员们搭建了更广阔的平台。

The Department of Outreach is the bridge between our organization and the outside world. They cultivate and maintain partnerships with sponsors, the university, embassies, and other student organizations, securing valuable resources and expanding our reach. Their efforts bring diverse opportunities and enrich our events, providing a broader platform for all members.
