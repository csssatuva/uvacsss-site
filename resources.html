Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì Academic Questions

How do I register for classes?

Use SIS (Student Information System) during your designated enrollment time. Check with your advisor for course recommendations.

Where can I get academic help?

Visit the Writing Center, attend professor office hours, and utilize campus academic resources.

How do I find research opportunities?

Reach out to professors in your field, check department websites, and attend research fairs.

üè† Living Questions

How do I open a bank account?

Visit a local bank branch with your passport, I-20/DS-2019, and proof of enrollment. Many banks offer student accounts.

What's the best phone plan?

Compare Verizon, AT&T, and T-Mobile plans. Many students prefer prepaid plans initially.

How do I get around without a car?

Use UVA buses for campus, CAT buses for the city, and Uber/Lyft for longer distances. Biking is also popular.
